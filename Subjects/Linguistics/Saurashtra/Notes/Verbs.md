## Modal Verbs

A modal verb is an auxiliary verb used to conjugate other verbs.

| English                 | Saurashtra       |
| ----------------------- | ---------------- |
| I ==should== have gone. | Mí jí'==rhano==  | 
| I ==would== have gone.  | Mí jí'==rhavu==  |
| I ==could== have gone.  | Mí jí'==rhavái== |
